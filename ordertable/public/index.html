Reservations

In order to make a reservation on behalf of someone else, you must register your account as an Administrative Professional. This feature allows executive assistants, travel planners, recruiters, and other administrative professionals to easily make reservations on behalf of others and earn valuable Dining Points.

Activating your account

Once you activate your account, you will receive a link to your message in your account info (or other method, if available). Bring your login and password to login into the system and find restaurants in order to apply it toward your meal at the restaurant. You can print out your location by clicking the “Show or location by Google map” link in display it from the location tab of this website.
